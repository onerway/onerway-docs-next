## Design your no-code integration

Understand which no-code solutions can help your business. You can use Onerway without writing any code. This guide helps you identify the right no-code products and services for your business.

Before using any of these products or solutions, you must register an Onerway account and submit the website where you plan to use payments. After registration, you can access the Dashboard to manage and configure your account and business.

## E-commerce platform integrations

The table below shows the documentation for configuring Onerway payments on e-commerce platforms without coding.

You don't need to download all the documentation—just select what you need. As your business grows, Onerway will add more partners and update this list.

### Shopify

**Shopify** supports two main categories of payment methods: **Card-only payments** and **Local aggregated payments**.

#### Card-only payments

Two flexible integration options:

- **Redirect checkout**: Users are redirected to the Onerway checkout page to complete payment
- **Embedded checkout**: Users can enter card details directly on the checkout page without redirection

#### Local aggregated payments

An integrated payment solution that supports both card payments and various local payment methods. During checkout, users are redirected to the Onerway checkout page where they can choose card or local payment options.

::note{icon="i-heroicons-information-circle"}
You must provide your Shopify store's original URL to use Shopify payments.
::

### Shoplazza

**Shoplazza** offers two payment integration options:

#### Card-only payments (Embedded)

Payment flow is seamlessly integrated into your store's checkout page. Customers can securely enter card information directly without leaving the page, providing a smooth and convenient experience.

#### Local payments (Aggregated)

An integrated solution supporting both card and popular local payment methods. During checkout, customers are redirected to the Onerway checkout page where they can choose their preferred payment method.

::note{icon="i-heroicons-information-circle"}
You must provide your Shoplazza store's original URL to use Shoplazza payments.
::

### Shopline

**Shopline** offers two payment integration options:

#### Card-only payments (Embedded)

Payment flow is fully embedded in your store's checkout page. Customers can securely enter card information without redirection, ensuring a consistent and smooth payment experience.

#### Local payments (Aggregated)

This solution integrates card payments with various regional mainstream payment methods. During checkout, customers are redirected to the Onerway unified checkout page, where they can choose credit cards, debit cards, or local payment tools.

::note{icon="i-heroicons-information-circle"}
You must provide your Shopline store's original URL to use Shopline local payments.
::

### Shopyy

**Shopyy** offers two flexible payment integration options:

#### Embedded checkout

Provides an extremely smooth payment experience for card users. Customers can securely complete transactions on the checkout page without leaving your store, effectively improving order conversion rates.

#### Aggregated checkout

Connects international card payments with mainstream global local payment methods through the Onerway checkout page, helping your store easily reach a wider customer base and meet payment preferences in different markets.

::note{icon="i-heroicons-information-circle"}
You need to select options in the Shopyy backend according to your needs. For card-only payments, select "Embedded"; for local payments, select "Checkout" mode.
::

### WooCommerce

When using payments with **WordPress** and **WooCommerce**, two integration options are available:

#### Default checkout

Download and install the payment plugin first, then configure it according to the documentation to complete the basic setup. Your store will then default to using our checkout interface. Customers will be redirected to the checkout page during payment.

#### Extended payment interface

If your business needs to integrate e-wallets, bank transfers, and other local payment methods, you can connect to our payment interface. This interface integrates richer payment options to meet diverse customer needs. This requires some development skills—refer to our [API documentation](https://docs.onerway.com/apis) for details.

### Shoplus

**Shoplus** offers the following integration option:

#### Embedded checkout

Provides an extremely smooth payment experience for card users. Customers can securely complete transactions on the checkout page without leaving the store, effectively improving order conversion rates.

## Sell and get paid online

Accept payments by creating payment links—no independent website required.

- Share the same link via email
- Embed payment links as buy buttons on your existing website
- Customize payment page appearance in the Dashboard
