## Accept payments

### Sell and get paid online

Create payment links and share them on any channel to easily collect payments.

**Key features:**

- No coding required
- Mobile-optimized checkout
- Multiple payment methods
- Automatic receipts
- Real-time notifications
- Customizable branding

**How it works:**

1. **Log into your Onerway Dashboard**
2. **Navigate to Payment Links**
3. **Click "Create Payment Link"**
4. **Configure your payment:**
  - Product/service name
  - Amount (or allow custom amounts)
  - Currency and description
5. **Share your link** via email, social media, or embed on your website

### Subscription hosting

Enable subscription services with automatic recurring payments.

Through the Onerway hosted page, provide customers with complete subscription billing lifecycle support:

- **Billing management**: View complete subscription lifecycle and billing history
- **Payment management**: Flexibly change payment methods
- **Subscription control**: Self-service subscription cancellation

### E-commerce platforms

For mainstream e-commerce platforms like Shopify, Shoplazza, and Shopline, you can configure Onerway payments directly on the platform after registering an Onerway account.
